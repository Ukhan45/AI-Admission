Project: AI Admission System

Stack: Next.js 14, Supabase (auth + DB), Groq/LLaMA AI, Tailwind CSS
Deployed: ai-admission-eta.vercel.app (GitHub: Ukhan45/AI-Admission)
Payments: Lemon Squeezy — PKR 800/month Pro plan
Webhook: app/api/webhook/lemonsqueezy/route.ts → updates profiles.plan = 'pro'

Supabase Tables:

profiles — id, email, plan, sop_used, analyzer_used, chat_used, credits_used, credits_limit, created_at
generations — id, user_id, type, university, content, created_at

Free Plan Limits:

SOP: 3 | Analyzer: 2 | Chat: 10

Pages:

/dashboard — stats, plan limits, recent generations
/sop-generator — generates SOP, uses sop_used
/profile-analyzer — profile score, uses analyzer_used
/university-finder — search with filters (no credit gate)
/chatbot — AI chat, uses chat_used
/checkout — pricing page (Free vs Pro)
/sop-history — past generations
